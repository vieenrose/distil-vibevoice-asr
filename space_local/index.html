Accuracy and privacy are the point of running locally — pick q4 only if download size matters. Reference decode speeds: 8–25 tok/s CPU (a 30 s clip ≈ 5–15 s of compute; an hour of meeting audio can take from tens of minutes up to a few hours of browser compute depending on your hardware — the transcript streams as it goes and can be stopped at any time, keeping partial results).
